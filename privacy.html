Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Proximate collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (such as your name, email, and preferences) and data automatically (such as your location and device details) to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to personalize matches, recommend nearby services, and ensure app security. We may also use non-personal data for analytics.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your data. We only share information with trusted service providers necessary for operating the app or when required by law.

4. Location Data

Proximate relies on location information to connect you with nearby users and services. You can disable location access in your device settings at any time, but certain features may not function properly.

5. Data Security

We implement strong encryption and access controls to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure. Use caution when sharing personal details with others.

6. Your Rights

You can request deletion, correction, or export of your data by contacting us through the app’s support section.

7. Updates to Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect app improvements or legal requirements. The effective date will be updated accordingly.
